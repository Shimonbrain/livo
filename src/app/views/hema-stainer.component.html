Quality and speed that keep pace with your sample loads

With the Livo HemaStainer, gone are the days when your only option for processing blood samples was relying on specialised manual labor, which often results in extended turnaround times that delay patient care and might even be a luxury for healthcare practices that operate on tight budgets.

Designed with the perishable nature of blood products in mind, this compact, fully automated device measures only 31.5 x 28 x 18cm and allows you to stain any number of blood smears with unrivalled speed and precision, eliminating idle time and the risk of cross contamination.
