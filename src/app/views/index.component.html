Why Livo Pathology Equipment?

Our automated pathology equipment is different from traditional equipment used for the analysis of blood tissue samples to examine and diagnose disease. We provide versatile and adaptable pathology equipment for your lab's needs.

We look at the workplace of pathology doctors, nurses, and technologists and see it get bogged down by yesterday's technology when they need to diagnose cancer, dementia, diabetes and various other diseases as quickly and accurately as possible. Our primary focus is to constantly explore innovative ways to improve workflow in diagnostics labs.

We designed pathology equipment around an imaging platform with integrated data systems providing fast, image-guided diagnosis to aid in the critical care of patients.

Some of our pathology equipment that brings healthcare innovation to get more accurate results and affordable healthcare are:

A700

The Livo A700 performs monolayer identification, cell identification, classification and differential counting with precision, relieving you of the most laborious part of blood smear analysis.

A1000

The Whole Slide Scanner Livo A1000 scans the processed slides in seconds, then uploads high-quality, digital images of them to the Livo digital pathology platform and sends them out for expert analysis.

Hematology Rapid Stain

This stain will give multiple colors and differential hues of the same color to different cellular components resulting in a clear contrasting field of view to make the cell differentials task easier. It gives you results in 60 secs, which is 10 times quicker than any other stain
