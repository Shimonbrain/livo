Why Livo Digital Radiology?

Our teleradiology service enhances patient care by removing delays in interpretation of images. It also offers followup after a diagnosis has been made. Other benefits include:

  • 24/7 access to a global network of vetted radiologists
  • Ability to scan and send high-quality images anywhere in the world
  • A useful second opinion on a previous diagnosis
  • Reduced overhead costs associated with employing a full-time radiologist if you are just starting your practice and have a limited budget
  • Instant review of images and swift diagnosis

With our digital radiology platform, we bring the world of medical diagnosis to you in one click!
