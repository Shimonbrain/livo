Livo A1000

The Whole Slide Scanner Livo A1000 is the most practical and compact digital slide scanner on the market. Designed to produce state-of-the-art pathology scans at the push of a button, it eliminates the laborious process of sitting down for hours behind a microscope to analyse results.

The Livo A1000 scans the processed slides in seconds, then uploads high-quality, digital images of them to the Livo digital pathology platform and sends them out for expert analysis.

Affordable whole slide imaging

Why Livo A1000?

The A1000 was envisioned to accelerate the workflow of the time-pressed, cost-conscious laboratory manager. Its benefits include:

  • High-resolution images
  • Scanning time as low as 60 seconds
  • Sustained high throughput rate
  • No rescans necessary
  • Compact, noiseless design that fits into small laboratory spaces
  • Access to a global network of certified pathologists
  • Compatible with hospital and lab proprietary information systems
  • Relief from neck/back pain and eye strain for staff pathologists

  • Higher turnaround time - Low throughput and reporting speed

  • Slides cannot be instantly shared for viewing

A1000

  • Lower turnaround time - High throughput and reporting speed
  • Easy storage, cataloging, and management of images
  • High-resolution images that do not alter in quality
  • Global network of certified pathologists available 24/7
  • Convenient, no back pain or eye strain
  • Digital slides can be easily shared with specialists who have access to the Livo platform
