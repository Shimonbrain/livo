Why Livo Digial Pathology?

Using high-precision diagnostic software, our telepathology services provide quality evaluation and review of slides. Other benefits of the service include:

  • High-resolution scanning (H&E - Histopathology, IHC - Histopathology, Cytopathology, Hematology)
  • Scan and send slide images seamlessly from our A-1000 scanner to anywhere in the world
  • Eliminate delays due to staff holidays, sick leave, or other incidental absences
  • Follow up on cases as needed even after reporting and diagnosis have concluded
  • Save time by digitising slides in seconds
  • View images in high resolution and at your desired magnification
