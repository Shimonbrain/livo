Shipping and delivery

1. Log into your Livo account

2. Go to Orders

3. You can track your shipment and delivery status for each order item upto date

4. We will start shipping product one payment was paid by you

5. Delivery charges varies based on location

6. Within india we will deliver the product within 3-7 working days

Cancellations and Returns

You can raise a request to return your items with these simple steps:

1. Log into your Livo account

2. Go to Orders

3. Click on 'Return/Cancel' against the item you wish to return or replacement

4. Fill in the details and raise a return request

Once you raise a request, you'll get an email that your request is being processed. Based on the item, your request may be automatically approved or you may be contacted for more details. If the request is approved, the item will be picked up after which you will get a replacement or refund. You can also track the status of your return request instantly from the 'My Orders' section of your Livo account.

5. Refund will be credit within 5-7 working days from the date of cancellation
